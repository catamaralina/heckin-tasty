Speculaas

Speculaas

Effort level: not much

Makes 1-2 dozen

Note: Makes an excellent gluten free pie crust

Ingredients

  • 2-5 Tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/2 tsp ground aniseed
  • 1/2 tsp ground white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground corriander

Optional

  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp ground mace
  • 1/2 tsp ground dried mandarin zest

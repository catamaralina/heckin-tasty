Studio Ghibli: Ponyo Drink

Effort level: Not much, 5 min

Makes 1

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp honey
  • pinch cinnamon
  • 1/2 mug boiling water
  • 1/2 mug warm milk

Directions

  1. Add honey, cinnamon and boiling water to mug and stir.
  2. Fill mug to top with warm milk and stir.

Captain Palmer

Effort level: none, 5 min

Makes 1

Ingredients

  • 3 oz brewed tea
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1-2 oz Captain Morgan Rum
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • Alternative: Instead of tea, honey and lemon juice, use a mixed iced tea lemonade (Palmer)

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients together and stir.
  2. Pour in glass filled with ice.

Shirley Temple

Effort level: if you've got the ingredients, 5 min

Makes some

Ingredients

  • Grenadine (red stuff)
  • Orange juice (with or without pulp: dealers choice)
  • Ginger ale or Sprite or SevenUp
  • Optional: Coconut Rum or Vodak (personally prefer Pinacle Whipped Vodka)

Directions

  1. Mix one part orange juice and one part Gingerale - enough to fill a glass with ice.
  2. Optional: stir in alcohol of choice.
  3. Pour in splash of grenadine, carefully to make a 'sunset'.

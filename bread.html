Rye

Effort level: , min

Makes 4

Ingredients

  • 2 c flour
  • 2 c bran
  • 1 1/2 red river
  • 1/3 c molasses
  • 2 1/2 - 2 3/4 c warm water
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 tsp baking soda

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375° F
  2. Mix and pour into large loaf pan and cover with tin foil.
  3. Bake for 1 - 1 1/2 hrs.
